Our Initiatives

E

Environmental Stewardship

Tree planting, soil conservation, water catchment systems, and training in sustainable farming to restore degraded land and increase food security.

Ed

Education & Youth

Support for local primary schools, scholarships, adult literacy classes, and vocational training to build skills and long-term opportunities.

H

Health & Hygiene

Community health workshops, vaccination outreach support, clean water projects, and training of community health workers to improve public health outcomes.

Current Projects & Impact

Village Tree Planting & Nursery

Planted 5,000+ seedlings in erosion-prone areas and established a local nursery managed by women-led groups.

School Supplies & Teacher Training

Provided learning materials for the primary schools at Ardaita and its surrounding and organized teacher training on interactive, contextual lessons.

Community Health Outreach

Supported vaccination drives and trained 12 community health volunteers to monitor maternal and child health.
