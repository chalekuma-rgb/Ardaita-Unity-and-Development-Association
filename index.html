About Us

Who We Are

Ardaita Unity and Development Association (AUDA) is a community-driven charitable organization dedicated to fostering sustainable progress, equitable education, and accessible healthcare in the Ardaita region of West Arsi Zone, Oromia, Ethiopia.

Founded by members of the community, our mission is to create sustainable, locally owned programs that improve livelihoods and wellbeing. We coordinate with local leaders and partner organizations to ensure culturally appropriate and effective interventions.

Vision

To build a healthy, educated, environmentally sustainable, and economically empowered community where every individual has the opportunity to thrive with dignity and unity.

Mission

AUDA is committed to improving the quality of life in our community by promoting health, expanding education, protecting the environment, and supporting economic activities.

Core Values

  • Unity – Collective effort is the foundation of development.
  • Integrity – Transparency and accountability in all actions.
  • Compassion – Serving with empathy and prioritizing the vulnerable.
  • Empowerment – Building skills, knowledge, and economic opportunity.
  • Sustainability – Promoting long-term, responsible solutions.
  • Equity & Inclusion – Equal opportunities regardless of background.
  • Innovation – Creative, locally driven approaches to community challenges.

Our Initiatives

We design and implement community-led programs across five focus areas.

🌿

Environmental Stewardship

Community reforestation, sustainable water resource management, environment awareness workshops, and waste reduction initiatives.

📚

Education & Youth

Primary school support, vocational training, digital literacy classes, and educational resource distribution to build skills and long-term opportunities.

🏥

Health & Hygiene

Public health awareness campaigns, mental wellness support, preventive care education, vaccination outreach support, and medical resource facilitation.

📈

Economic Activities

Micro-finance group support, small business mentorship, entrepreneurship training, and agricultural development support to enhance household income and self-reliance.

🤝

Social Care

Protecting children and the elderly, supporting at-risk children, and assisting vulnerable elderly members of our community.

Current Projects & Impact

Village Tree Planting & Nursery

Planted 5,000+ seedlings in erosion-prone areas and established a local nursery managed by women-led groups.

School Supplies & Teacher Training

Provided learning materials for primary schools in Ardaita and its surrounding areas, and organized teacher training on interactive, contextual lessons.

Community Health Outreach

Supported vaccination drives and trained 12 community health volunteers to monitor maternal and child health.
